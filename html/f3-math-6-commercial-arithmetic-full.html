Simple Interest

Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal — it is constant each period.

I = PRT / 100    A = P + I
P = Principal  |  R = Rate (%) p.a.  |  T = Time (years)
Example: Find SI on Ksh 15 000 at 8% p.a. for 3 years.
I = (15000 × 8 × 3) / 100 = Ksh 3 600
A = 15000 + 3600 = Ksh 18 600
Find rate: Ksh 5 000 earns Ksh 900 SI in 3 years.
R = (I × 100) / (P × T) = (900 × 100) / (5000 × 3) = 6% p.a.

Compound Interest

Compound interest is calculated on the accumulated amount each period — interest earns interest.

A = P(1 + R/100)ⁿ
CI = A − P
n = number of compounding periods
Example: Ksh 20 000 at 10% p.a. compound interest for 3 years.
A = 20000 × (1.10)³ = 20000 × 1.331 = Ksh 26 620
CI = 26620 − 20000 = Ksh 6 620
Compare: SI = 20000 × 10 × 3 / 100 = Ksh 6 000 (less!)
Half-yearly compounding: Rate = 10% p.a. → 5% per half year; n = 2t
A = P(1 + 5/100)²ᵗ

Appreciation and Depreciation

Appreciation — value increases over time (e.g. land, antiques)
New Value = P(1 + R/100)ⁿ
Depreciation — value decreases over time (e.g. cars, machinery)
New Value = P(1 − R/100)ⁿ
Appreciation: Land worth Ksh 500 000 appreciates at 12% p.a. Value after 4 years:
V = 500000 × (1.12)⁴ = 500000 × 1.5735 = Ksh 786 764
Depreciation: Car bought at Ksh 800 000 depreciates at 15% p.a. Value after 3 years:
V = 800000 × (0.85)³ = 800000 × 0.6141 = Ksh 491 300

Hire Purchase

Hire Purchase (HP) allows buying goods by paying a deposit then monthly instalments. HP always costs more than cash price.

HP Cost = Deposit + (Monthly Instalment × No. of Months)
Extra Cost = HP Cost − Cash Price
Example: A TV costs Ksh 24 000 cash. On HP: deposit Ksh 4 000 + 12 monthly instalments of Ksh 2 200.
HP Cost = 4000 + (12 × 2200) = 4000 + 26400 = Ksh 30 400
Extra cost = 30400 − 24000 = Ksh 6 400
% Extra = (6400/24000) × 100 = 26.7%

Income Tax (PAYE)

Income tax is charged on taxable income using tax bands. PAYE = Pay As You Earn.

Example: Monthly gross income = Ksh 30 000. Relief = Ksh 1 056.
Band 1: 12298 × 10% = 1 229.80
Band 2: (23885−12298) × 25% = 11587 × 25% = 2 896.75
Band 3: (30000−23885) × 30% = 6115 × 30% = 1 834.50
Gross tax = 1229.80 + 2896.75 + 1834.50 = 5 961.05
PAYE = 5961.05 − 1056 = Ksh 4 905.05

Key Comparisons:

  • HP cost > Cash price always
  • Depreciation: car/machine value falls exponentially
  • To find number of years: use logarithms with CI formula
